Accessibility has historically been framed as a tradeoff against velocity. Teams who cared still paid a tax in planning, implementation, and review.

AI-assisted development changes that equation when used with intention.

If generated components include semantic structure, keyboard flow, and clear naming from the first draft, inclusive implementation becomes the lower-friction path. That is a strategic shift, not just a tooling upgrade.
