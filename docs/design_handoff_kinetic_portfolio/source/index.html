02 · The thesis

Accessibility isn't a feature. It's an architectural decision.

It determines whether a product serves everyone or only some. That has always been true. What's new is that AI-assisted development is the first thing to make the accessible version also the cheapest one.

That inversion is where my work lives now. Not remediation. Not compliance. The architecture of products where inclusion is the path of least resistance.
